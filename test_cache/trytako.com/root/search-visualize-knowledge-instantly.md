# Search & Visualize Knowledge, Instantly


Democratizing Data Access


## Content

Who uses Tako?
--------------

Tako delivers interactive up-to-date and shareable knowledge cards built exclusively from the world’s most authoritative data sources.

![Image 5](https://tako-static-assets-production.s3.amazonaws.com/archive/20f06ce365139df2b863194df8e607230834079f/images/v2/mind.svg)

### Knowledge workers

Tako's AI graphics empower your storytelling. Create and share compelling interactive knowledge cards using data from trusted sources. Our platform delivers reliable, impactful insights to elevate your content.

### Developers

Unlock the world’s authoritative knowledge with Tako’s API. Our advanced search and generative UI technology lets you deliver more accurate, timely and engaging answers to your users.

### Data Providers

Showcase your trusted insights to Tako’s network of developers and knowledge workers, amplifying your reach and impact in the industry.

### Consumers

We’re testing Tako Search to deliver you fast, precise, dynamic data visuals from trusted sources. Tako’s interactive knowledge cards turn data into visually stunning stories, ensuring your points hit home and stand out.
